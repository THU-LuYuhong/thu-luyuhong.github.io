Research Interests

My research interests lie at the convergence of artificial intelligence, power systems, and economics, with a focus on enabling the low-carbon transition of the energy sector. Key areas include:

  • Data-Driven Forecasting and Uncertainty Management for Modern Power Systems: Renewable Energy Power Forecasting; Short-term Load Prediction; Probabilistic Forecasting & Uncertainty Quantification; Big Data Analytics in Energy Systems.

  • Electricity Markets and Quantitative Trading Strategies: Electricity Price Forecasting; Market Clearing and Mechanism Design; Quantitative Trading and Risk Management; Carbon Market Analysis and Price Interaction.

  • Integrated Energy Systems & Demand-Side Optimization: Home Energy Management Systems; Demand Response & Flexible Load Scheduling; EV Charging Coordination & V2G Integration; Distributed Energy Resource Management.

  • AI-Enabled Smart Energy System: Large Language Models (LLMs) for Energy Systems; Explainable AI for Energy; Smart Meter Data Analytics and Load Monitoring; AI-driven Optimization for Adaptive Control.
